FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

HOW WILL I GET MY CME CERTIFICATE / POINTS?

Your certificate will be issued once you have completed your course evaluation form at the end of the course.

WHAT EQUIPMENT WILL BE USED DURING A COURSE?

The equipment used will depend on the nature of the course. Some courses are lecture based and some more practical. Please see the individual course details for further information.

CAN I PAY FOR MY COURSE ONLINE?

We are currently working on our online payment system which will be up and running very soon. In the meantime, cheque and bank transfer payments are accepted. All the details can be found on the invoice that will be emailed to you once you have registered your details for the course.

WILL I GET A REFUND IF THE COURSE I HAVE REGISTERED FOR IS CANCELLED?

If the course you have registered for is cancelled, we will speak with you to see if there are any other courses that you are interested in. If the course is rescheduled we will list you onto the next available date. If you are unable to attend any of the upcoming dates or courses we will refund the course fees to you.

I HAVE PAID TO ATTEND A COURSE BUT I AM UNABLE TO ATTEND

Our schedules and planning of courses is based on the people attending. If you are unable to attend a course and advise us with a minimum of two weeks’ notice we will issue you with a credit note that can be used for future courses. Any notice less than 2 weeks but more than 1 week would result in a credit note at 50% of the value paid. Any less than a week would mean no credit note would be issued if you are unable to attend.

IF I HAVE REGISTERED MY DETAILS FOR A COURSE AM I A CONFIRMED ATTENDEE?

Your place on the course will be confirmed once payment for the course has been received.

WHERE CAN I SEE MY COURSE HISTORY?

Your course history can be viewed by logging into your account using the details you entered when you registered for your first course.
