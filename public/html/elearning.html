E-LEARNING FOR HEALTHCARE PRACTITIONERS

Tri-c wants to revolutionize the way in which courses and trainings are delivered

When the instructional methods (how to present information in a way that is conducive) remain the same, so does the learning. Tri-C Academy wants to revolutionize the way in which courses and trainings are delivered, by transforming the instructional design and their delivery methods through our e-learning platform. Besides traditional face-to-face learning (which is still essential), we are great believers in using blended learning concepts, distance learning, and e-learning, which can help course attendees achieve much more (and longer retention) from our courses. We aim to help participants achieve lifelong learning. multi-lateral communication channels that span time and space. It will also allow for group and faculty interaction and convergence (Long Tail Learning) before, during, and after the courses.
