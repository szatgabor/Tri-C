THE HOME OF GOOD HEALTH

Bringing the best global continuing professional development home for health professionals

OUR LEARNING HUB

Tri-C, a medical training and innovation academy, is a place for continuous professional development for healthcare practitioners across all medical disciplines. Our state of the art training facility, conveniently located near some of the key Dubai landmarks and medical institutions, provides healthcare and medical education bespoke courses that will create a community of best practice in the region.


Partnerships with key global organizations will enable us to bring the most up to date courses to the region, allowing local access to global courses and educators, enabling us to enhance healthcare education and its delivery in the region and across the world.


We are using the latest innovative technology to ensure knowledge and skill retention which will include an e-learning platform which will include web-based learning and live surgical transmissions.


We are not only catering for the education of healthcare professionals, but will be a support and education centre for ordinary people who may be suffering from health issues, along with their family and friends who may also needs some support in helping their loved ones. We are also an educational hub for schools and colleges, giving a real insight into the medical field to our future healthcare professionals.


We are a community where everyone belongs.


OUR JOURNEY

Tri-C is the result of a group of dedicated and passionate professionals whose main purpose was to bring people together in one hub where they could connect, care and create.


We aim to create a community of best practice through bespoke interprofessional courses, and create positive change in the health care community by becoming the incubator for the practice of health care practitioners and educators in the region.


We welcome everyone to join our journey whether that be as our associates, partners, educators, students or simply curious minds.
