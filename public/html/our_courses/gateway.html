GATEWAY DEBRIEFING SKILLS WORKSHOP

2nd – 3rd March 2018

2 days

SPECIALITY

Health Professions Education

COURSE DATE

Saturday 31st March 2018

COURSE DURATION

1 day

TIME OF COURSE

8am – 5pm

LOCATION

Tri-C, Spectrum Building, Oud Metha, Dubai

COURSE FEE

AED 2,900 (including 5% VAT)

WHO SHOULD ATTEND

Health Professionals (Doctors, Nurses, Pharmacists, paramedics, anesthesiologists, and other healthcare workers who are involved in undergraduate and postgraduate education).

SUMMARY OF COURSE

For the first time in the UAE, TRI-C in collaboration with DHA is proud to bring the renowned team from The Center for Medical Simulation (CMS) in Boston, MA, USA, to conduct their world class workshop on Debriefing Skills .

If you are involved with the field of simulation as an educator , instructor, or simulation technicians ... This is the right course for you . If your institution is seeking a unique faculty development course, this is the course for you. This innovative workshop combines a day long immersive in-person experience with online learning that includes customized peer and mentor feedback.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Designed for busy healthcare educators with limited time, the workshop will enable the participants to do the following: Immerses participants in the deliberate practice of skilled debriefing Connects participants to a community of colleagues where they can

continually develop their feedback and bedside teaching skills Teaches participants to efficiently improve future performance Demonstrates setting high standards while regarding learners highly Enables the educate to treat their learners as thinkers Practice multiple phases of debriefing

ACCREDITATION

CME Accreditation will be applied to this course

COURSE FACULTY

Dr. Demian Szyld - Senior Director - Institute for Medical Simulation – Centre for Medical Simulation

Dr. Demian Szyld, MD, EdM is the Senior Director of the Institute for Medical Simulation and an Emergency Physician at the Brigham and Women’s Hospital. He trained in Emergency Medicine at the University of Pennsylvania and was the first Simulation and Education Fellow at the STRATUS Simulation Center.

Dr. Kate Morse - Assistant Director (Educational Leadership & International Programs)

Alongside her role as Associate Director, Dr. Kate Morse also holds a faculty appointment at Harvard Medical School and Massachusetts General Hospital. She is educated to PhD level and is also currently serving s the VP of Membership for INASCL (International Nursing Association in Simulation and Clinical Learning).
