LAPAROSCOPY IN UROLOGY

9th-10th March 2018

2 days

SPECIALITY

Urology

COURSE DATE

9-10 March 2018

COURSE DURATION

2 days

LOCATION

Tri-C, Spectrum Building, Oud Metha, Dubai

COURSE FEE

AED 2,100 (including VAT)

WHO SHOULD ATTEND

Urologists and general surgeons with some experience in urologic procedures. A basic knowledge of laparoscopy is also required.

SUMMARY OF COURSE

Laparoscopy is mini-invasive alternative to the robotic surgery, however the learning curve requires a vast knowledge a good knowledge of the following:

  • Patient preparation
  • Correct indications
  • Laparoscopic instruments
  • Major surgical technique - outlined step by step
  • Guidelines for each procedure
  • Postoperative management
  • Resolutions for frequent complications
  • Results and outcomes

This course is devised to cover all these topics in the context of major laparoscopic urological procedures.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

The objective of the course is provide the learner a thorough understanding of all the elements outlined in the course summary.

ACCREDITATION

CME Accreditation will be applied to this course

COURSE FACULTY

Franco Gaboardi, Chief Dept of Urology San Raffaele Hospital (Milan, Italy)

Dr. Franco Gaboardi is one of the pioneers of minimally invasive urological surgery in laparoscopic and robotic techniques. Specializing in Urology and Andrology he began his first laparoscopic experience in 1992 by performing laparoscopic lymphadenectomy in prostate cancer. In January 2001 he operated the first patient in Italy of laparoscopic radical prostatectomy and in September of the same year the first patient of laparoscopic radical cystectomy with neovascial ileal. Three years later he performed the first robotic radical prostatectomy in Italy and subsequently laparoscopic prostatectomy with only one umbilical access.

Salvatore Smelzo - Urologist at San Raffaele Hospital (Milan, Italy)

Dr. Salvatore Smelzo is currently working alongside Dr. Franco Gaboardi in the department of Urology at at San Raffaele Hospital in Milan. Prior to this he did his fellowship in Laparoscopic and Robotic Urology Surgery in France. He completed his residency in Urology in the University of Naples Federico and has published a number of articles on Morris Syndrome.

Nazareno Suardi - Urologist at San Raffaele Hospital (Milan, Italy)

Dr. Nazareno Suardi completed his degree in Medicine and Surgery in July 2003 from the Vita-Salute San University Raffaele. Following his graduation he enrolled in the School of Specialization in Urology which was followed by his fellowship and clinical fellowship after which he obtained his title from the European Board of Urology.
